About us

Our teachers are active members of their music communities and have occupied a unique place in music education, and mentoring students from 5–99. We are dedicated to the highest standards here at MusicSOL, and we understand the importance of quality service.

The instructors in our network create their own lesson plans based on what is best for you and your child.

Sign up today to have our knowledgeable staff assist you.

Learn in the comfort and safety of your own home. All lessons currently take place online. We have no binding contracts. We want you to always enjoy the lessons with your teacher. If something changes, you are free to cancel or change teachers at any time.

Start your musical journey today!

How it works?

  1. Tell Us Your Needs
    We’ll ask you a few questions so we can put you in touch with the most fitting teachers in your area.
  2. We Assign You to a Teacher
    We will put you in touch with a teacher that best matches your needs.
  3. Book Your Lesson
    Your assigned teacher will contact you to set up your first lesson.

Lessons
