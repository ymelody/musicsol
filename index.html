About us

MusicSOL 

(pronounced myo͞oziksōl, SOL being the 5th solfege scale degree, similar sound to soul.) 

MusicSOL teachers are active members of their music communities and have occupied a unique place in music education. We operate on a global scale and have teachers from around the world, including Italy, Spain, Israel, Greece, England, Canada, United States and more! 

MusicSOL is an online conservatory-style music learning program. Our students not only receive lessons on their instrument/s of choice, but also receive instruction in music theory. 

The instructors in our network create their own unique lesson plans based on what is best suited for each individual student.

Sign up today to have our knowledgeable staff assist you. 

COVID19 precautions: Learn in the comfort and safety of your own home. All lessons currently take place online. We have no binding contracts. We want you to always enjoy the lessons with your teacher. If something changes, you are free to cancel or change teachers at any time.
